This tutorial will show you how to access your GeForce NOW account through Interstellar.
---
1. Open GeForce NOW.
2. Sign in.
3. Close the sign in tab.
4. Reload the original tab.
5. Find a game and click `Play`.
---
### Note: This works only on PC

<Steps titleSize="h3">
<Step title="How to fix Google results being buggy ?">
Go in Interstellar settings and change your default browser to Bing for example
</Step>
<Step title="How do I login to GeForce NOW?">
See ["Accessing GeForce NOW"](/geforcenow) for detailed step-by-step instructions
</Step>

<Step title="Some games are not working for me">
Try to change the proxy in settings, this might work for some specific games
</Step>

<Step title="How do I clear my cache?">
For Safari iPad users, [see this guide](https://support.apple.com/en-ca/105082). For Microsoft Edge users, [see this guide instead](https://www.microsoft.com/en[..])
</Step>
</Steps>

Why: Sometimes things break on Interstellar, clearing your cache might fix some (but not all) common issues.

### Q: How do I clear my cache?
A: For Google Safari iPad users, [see this guide](https://support.apple.com/en-ca/105082). For Microsoft Edge users, [see this guide instead](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/edge/learning-center/how-to-manage-and-clear-your-cache-and-cookies).

Why: Sometimes things break on Interstellar, clearing your cache might fix some (but not all) common issues.

<Warning>If [cyclic.sh](https://cyclic.sh/) is blocked, the links are going to be blocked.</Warning>

## Step 1 
Visit [the interstellar repo](https://github.com/InterstellarNetwork/IN-Render) and fork the repository

## Step 2 
Visit [cyclic.sh](https://cyclic.sh/) and login with Github.
Click **Link Your Own** and choose the Github Repo you just forked.
Click **Connect Cyclic** and give it access to your repos.

## Step 3
Wait for it to deploy, and then you have a working link.

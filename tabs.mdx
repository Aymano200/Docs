**This tutorial will guide you through all aspects of Interstellar's tab system.**
## Show/hide the tab bar
Don't like the new tab bar? You can hide or show it by clicking on the magnifying glass at the top right of your screen.

This does not close your tabs, it only hides the tab bar.

## Open a new tab
Click on the `+` in the tab bar to open a new tab.

## Close an open tab
To close a tab, click on the `X` next to the tab title.

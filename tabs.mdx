**This tutorial will walk you through every aspect of the Interstellar tab system.**
## Showing/hiding the tab bar
Don't like the new tab bar? You can hide or unhide it by clicking the magnifying glass in the top right corner of your screen.

This does not close your tabs, this only hides the tab bar.
